Space is Nearly Silent

Since sound waves need a medium to travel through, space, being a vacuum, is almost entirely silent. Astronauts use radio waves to communicate.

There are More Stars Than Grains of Sand

It's estimated that the universe contains more stars than there are grains of sand on all the beaches on Earth. That's a truly staggering number!

The Largest Planet is Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system. It's so big that you could fit all the other planets inside it.

A Day on Venus is Longer Than a Year

Venus has a very slow axial rotation. A single day on Venus (the time it takes to spin once on its axis) is longer than its orbital period around the Sun (its year).
