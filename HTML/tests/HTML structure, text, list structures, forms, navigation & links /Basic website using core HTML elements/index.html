How to bake a cake?

  1. Gather all your ingredients and preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour your cake pan to prevent sticking

    ingredients

    • 1 cup white sugar
    • ½ cup unsalted butter
    • 2 large eggs
    • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
    • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 ¾ teaspoons baking powder
    • /½ cup milk
  2. Cream together sugar and butter until fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, beating after each addition. Mix in vanilla extract. In a separate bowl, combine flour and baking powder, then gradually add to the wet mixture. Stir in milk until smooth
  3. Pour the batter into the prepared pan. Bake in the oven for about 30-40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Remove from oven and let the cake cool completely before frosting or serving
